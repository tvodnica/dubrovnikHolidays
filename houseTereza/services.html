Elaphiti islands tour

Embark on an exciting and memorable sea adventure! Explore the beauty of three magnificant Elaphiti islands - Koločep, Lopud and Šipan. Escape the city traffic to swim and snorkell in clear blue seas, discover hidden bays and sea caves, sunbathe on sandy or rocky beaches, and explore small villages. Book your trip on an orginized tour on the big boat or go alone on a small boat with the skipper.
