This was the perfect holiday location for me: You walk out of the back door and about six feet away is the sea! The water is clean and calm and it is great fun to swim across the bay to the other side. If you like to sunbathe there are plenty of sunbeds (enough for all the guests) and plenty of shade (including a tree where I liked to spend my time relaxing and reading). Zaton is a great location for someone who wants peace and quiet. There are just a handful of restaurants (which are all very good) but no bars or large hotels full of rowdy guests. However, if you want nightlife and crowds, Dubrovnik is just a bus ride away. So you have the best of both worlds. Perfect for me and my kids (who like swimming and relaxing) and my partner (who likes going out).
