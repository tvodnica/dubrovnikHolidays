General information


The local public bus company is called Libertas. All their buses are orange.

One ticket from Zaton to Dubrovnik or vice versa costs 2,30 Euro.
One ticket for city buses costs 2,00 Euro.

The tickets are bought on the bus in cash.
Every bus must be entered using the first door.

Bus timetable

Shown below is a custom bus timetable for House Tereza. Keep in mind that none of the buses depart from Zaton, so the shown times for Zaton are approximate. The times when buses depart back from Dubrovnik are exact.

The timetable is subject to change without prior notice, so for an official and guaranteed up to date timetable visit the bus company's official website.

Directions from Zaton to Dubrovnik

1. Get to the bus stop

When you reach the road on the parking at House Tereza, go right. Follow the road to the bus stop. It takes 5 minutes of walking.

2. Board the bus

Enter the bus on the front door. Buy the ticket at the driver and pay in cash. The ticket costs 2,30 Euro.

3. Enjoy the ride

Driving will take 10-15 minutes. On your way you will go across the bridge. After the bridge MOST buses will continue forward and go throught the city before reaching the main bus terminal. However, some buses will go directly to the main bus terminal and not go throughout the city. Please refer to the timetable to see where your bus goes.

  • My bus goes:
  • Throughout city
  • Directly to main bus terminal

4. Get off the bus

Get off the bus at the bus stop afer a short tunnel which you will go through along the way. For the exact location of the bus stop on Google maps click here. From here you can walk to the Old town. It takes 10-15 minutes of walking and it is all downhill.

5. Cross the road

Go along the road down the hill until you reach an underpass. Use it to cross the road. Exit using the stairs at the end of the underpass.

6. Walk to the Old town

Continue walking in the now opposite direction than earlier (go right after exiting the underpass). Continue walking following the road for 7-8 minutes until you reach the city walls. Now go down the stairs to the right until you reach Pile gate and entrance to the Old town.

4. Get off the bus

Get off the bus at the main bus terminal. This is the only stop after the bridge, so you cannot miss it.

5. Get to the city bus stop

Cross the road to reach the main road. Walk to your right until you reach the bus stop for city buses.

6. Board a city bus

Board a bus with number 1A, 1B, 1C or 3. Enter the bus at the front door and buy the ticket at the bus driver in cash.

7. Get off

Get off the bus at Pile gate in front of the Old town. Once again this is the last stop and you cannot miss it.
