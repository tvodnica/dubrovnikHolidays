Elaphiti islands boat tour

Explore the beauty of three magnificant Elaphiti islands - Koločep, Lopud and Šipan. Escape the city traffic to swim and snorkel in clear blue seas, discover hidden bays and sea caves, sunbathe on sandy or rocky beaches, and explore small villages.

What will you see?

What is shown below is a standard route which is usually taken. However this is a private boat tour and there are no other guests onboard, so this means that you can choose where to go, what to skip and how long to stay where.

1. Blue cave

Dive underwater and enter the famous blue cave to swim in the clear blue water. We will provide all the snorkels, so don't worry about that. The cave is only reachable by a boat, so don't miss out!

2. Three green caves

Just a super short travel from the Blue cave there are 3 additional green caves. The first one is big and easy, just swim inside and warm up for the next ones! The second cave is more interesting as it is darker and smaller. The third cave is a long and narrow channel which you can go thought and exit on the other side and then come back swimming around. It is necessary to dive a couple of times to go through. At one point you can even dive off the course to your right and dive out in a small cave with shallow sand bottom and no roof.

3. Koločep

A small fishing village where you can walk around and explore. There is a small shop and a coffe bar by the sea.

4. Šunj beach

The most famous sandy beach in Dubrovnik, located on the island of Lopud. It has a big shallow area which makes is great for kids. There are a couple of bars and restaurants, sun beds and umbrellas, and paddle cars to rent. There are also golf carts which can take you to the other side of the island.

5. Lopud village

Explore the other side of the Lopud island. If you didn't take that golf cart tour, then we just docked in the small harbour in the village. From here you can visit the Dominican and Franciscan monasteries, Đorđić-Mayneri Park and small churches. Or you can enjoy some more swimming and snorkeling on other smaller sandy beaches. Here you can also have lunch at one of the restaurants by the sea which we can book for you.

6. Šipan

The largest Elaphiti island. Here you can explore another small fishing village called Suđurađ. Or we can take you to the famous Bowa restaurant situated in one of Šipan's countless beautiful bays.

7. Sikirica

Before the end of the tour a must see is the Sikirica cove which offers a piece of paradise for swimming and snorkeling. Clear blue sea with shallow and rich sea vegetation makes it a perfect place to swim and snorkel, just before returning to the bow of the boat to enjoy a glass of your favourite drink before the sun sets down on the horizon.
