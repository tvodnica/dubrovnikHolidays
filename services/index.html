Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens to my reservation in case of bad weather?

    In case of bad weather all reservations will be cancelled. You will have an option to either rebook for a different date or receive a full refund.

  • Is it possible to pay with a credit card?

    It is possible to pay by a credit card, but in that case it is necessary to pay an additional fee of 5% of the original amount.

  • What is the cancellation policy?

    You are welcome to cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before the tour starts and get a full refund, with no questions asked.

  • Are shown prices per person or per tour/rental?

    All displayed prices are per tour or rental regardless of number of people.

  • How far are we allowed to kayak?

    You are allowed to go as far as you would like, just make sure you will have enough energy to paddle back. Also make sure to come back before dark because kayak has no lights.

  • My question is not in the FAQ. What do I do?

    If you still have any questions feel free to contact us.
