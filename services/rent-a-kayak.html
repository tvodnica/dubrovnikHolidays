Where to?

You are allowed to go anywhere you want. Shown below are some of the closer recommendations. If going outside the bay or to the islands, make sure to always give way to larger vessels and mind the wind and waves to make sure you can safely return.

1. Zaton Veliki beach

A nice pebble beach overlooking the bay entrance. Has safety buoys for swimmers where you can tie the kayak.

2. Štikovica beach

Again, a pebble beach overlooking the bay with safety buoys for swimmers where you can tie the kayak.

3. Tri brata beach

Right at the exit of the bay. Only reachable by sea, so rarely crowded.

4. Klačine bay

A nice small bay in the shade of trees. Has no buoys to moore on.

5. Vrbica beach

A nice small beach without big crowds.
