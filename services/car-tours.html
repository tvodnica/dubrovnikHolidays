Where can you go?

1. Pelješac

A magnificent peninsula with plenty of picturesque vineyards where you can enjoy winery tours and tastings, as well as oyster and mussel tastings in excellent seafood restaurants. Visit small town of Ston famous for it's salt and oyster production as well as it's long defensive walls, the second largest in the world, shorther just than the great Chinese wall. On the way back a stop can be made in Trsteno where you can visit the magnificent arboretum.

2. Bosnia

Discover the fascinating history and unique culture of Mostar, witness the spiritual energy of Međugorje, and marvel at the natural wonder of Kravice waterfalls on our captivating tour. Immerse yourself in the rich history and natural beauty of these iconic Bosnian destinations and create unforgettable memories.

This tour crosses the border, passports are required!
